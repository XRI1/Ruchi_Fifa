Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about the Ruchi Campaign? Here is everything you need to know.

How do I register?

+
Registration is simple. Head over to the Registration Page, enter your Name, Phone, Email, and Area. Upload a profile image, choose the country you support, accept the Terms and submit the form. Once registered, your high scores in the games will be recorded on the leaderboard!

Can I change my supporting country?

+
Yes. You can edit your country choice by returning to the Registration Page (which will show your success card if registered), clicking "Edit Details", selecting another country, and updating. Any new scores you record will support your new country.

How are scores calculated?

+
Your points are earned in the Penalty Shootout challenge:
  • Penalty Shootout: Scoring regular goals adds 100 points. Scoring in the top corner targets adds 150 points. Saves by the goalie block your shot streak.
Your Personal Best score forms your Individual Leaderboard score.

How many attempts can I make?

+
You can play the challenge as many times as you like! There are no limits to the number of attempts. Keep playing to beat your personal best and send your supporting team flying up the standings.
